FAQ Section
Questions, Answers
Get quick answers to your most pressing questions

What services do you offer?

I specialize in end-to-end product design — from UX research and wireframing to high-fidelity UI design and interactive prototyping. I also offer branding, design systems, and dev-ready handoff using Figma, Webflow, and Framer.

What industries do you work with?

I've worked across e-commerce, healthtech, content platforms, legal tech, and AI. I focus on companies looking to scale quickly and build intuitive digital products that drive user growth and engagement.

What's your design process like?

I start by understanding your users and business goals. Then I move into wireframing, visual exploration, and rapid iteration using feedback loops. I deliver pixel-perfect interfaces with clear documentation and dev-ready assets.

Do you work with developers or teams?

Yes — I've worked closely with in-house and remote dev teams, product managers, founders, and marketing leads. I speak both design and product fluently and thrive in cross-functional environments.

What tools do you use?

Figma (main), Adobe CC, Webflow, Framer, FigJam, Notion, Slack, Maze, and more. I'm also familiar with motion tools and 3D modeling for immersive interfaces.

What's your availability and rate?

I offer flexible engagements — from fixed-scope projects to long-term retainers. My rate reflects 8+ years of UX/UI experience delivering launch-ready products. Let's chat about your scope and timeline.

Can you show me relevant past work?

Yes — I have case studies available for select projects including Sanctify, Futuri, Karma, and Wnder. I'm happy to walk you through relevant examples based on your industry and needs.

Do you offer branding or strategy too?

Absolutely. I believe UX/UI doesn't exist in a vacuum. I often partner with brands at an early stage to shape their product positioning, voice, and identity alongside interface design.

Why should I work with you instead of an agency?

I move faster, collaborate more closely, and care deeply about your product's success. I bring both architectural thinking and modern design sensibility to every project — minus the agency overhead.

How do we get started?

Message me with your project idea, and I'll schedule a 20-minute discovery call to explore fit, timeline, and outcomes. If we're aligned, I'll send over a proposal within 48 hours.
